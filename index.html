Summary

This web page is maintained by Keisuke Otaki, a researcher at Toyota Central R&D Labs., Inc. His primary research interests include:

  • Data-centric research on human mobility data.
  • Applications of mathematical methods -- including modeling, solver/algorithm development, and information system design-- to real-world problems.
  • Applied methods, including optimization, machine learning, LLM agents, and more, for the emerging “AI for Science” era.
